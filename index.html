What is MyReportPlz..?

MyReportPlz is a BPaaS Solution for the Pathology Labs. The solution enables a Pathology Lab to upload Patients Test Reports on the cloud, that is, accessible to Patients and the Doctors from their laptops, desktop, mobile phones and tablets / iPad. Patient gets instant notification via SMS about ready reports.

MyReportPlz App is a Free solution for the patients to view the Reports via different modes that include a Web Portal, Mobile App for iOS and Android (available on Mobile and Tablet / iPad devices). It simply requires a Quick Registration of the patient that takes less than a minute to signup. Reports uploaded on the cloud are available to the patient for life time.
